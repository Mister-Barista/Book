Prologue

Our Story, Told Through Music

Every love story has a soundtrack.

This is mine.

These aren't just songs—they're moments. Every one of them reminds me of a feeling, a conversation, a smile, or a memory that changed me. Together, they tell the story of how I fell in love with you.

The Day We Met

When we first met, something about you immediately caught my attention.

I couldn't explain it, but every conversation left me smiling. I'd play songs that filled me with energy because they reminded me of you. I remember thinking, "I'm so glad I met her."

You drove me absolutely wild, but it wasn't just attraction. I found myself imagining what "us" could become. I wanted to pull you aside and kiss you... but I also knew something this special deserved patience. I wanted to get to know you properly.

You Became My Safe Place

We'd both been hurt before.

Even so, every time I saw you, my whole day became brighter. If work was stressful or life felt heavy, all I had to do was think about you, and somehow everything became lighter.

You didn't even know it, but you were carrying me through days you weren't there to see.

Before long, bringing you your morning coffee became one of my favourite parts of the day. It wasn't about the coffee anymore, it was about seeing you smile.

Every quiet morning before work became filled with thoughts of you. When work became busy, I'd find myself searching for those thoughts again because they gave me peace.

You became the calm in my chaos.

You Helped Me Find Myself Again

As I got to know you, something unexpected happened.

A part of me I'd buried for a long time started coming back to life.

I realised how much love, compassion and gentleness I still had inside me. You weren't asking me to change, you simply made me feel safe enough to become myself again.

Friends noticed how highly I spoke of you.

They could see what I was trying to hide.

The truth was... I was already falling.

You were putting me back together without even knowing it.

Falling For Your Soul

One night I realised something.

I didn't even know what you looked like.

And somehow... it didn't matter.

Your kindness.

Your humour.

Your heart.

The way we understood each other.

That was enough.

I wasn't falling for a face.

I was falling for your soul.

Saying "Yes" Before You Even Asked

The more we talked, the more certain I became.

If one day you asked me to be yours...

My answer would already be yes.

Not because everything was perfect.

But because of the connection we had built together.

I believed in us.

Learning Your Heart

As I listened to you, I realised your heart had walls around it.

Not because you didn't want love...

But because life had given you reasons to protect yourself.

I never wanted to tear those walls down.

I simply wanted to stand beside them until one day you felt safe enough to open the gate yourself.

I wanted to be the person who held your heart gently.

When I Thought I Was Losing You

Then things became uncertain.

Life became overwhelming.

You needed space.

Every part of me wanted to fight for us, but love isn't about holding someone too tightly.

So I stepped back.

Not because my feelings changed...

But because loving someone sometimes means giving them room to breathe.

I'll admit...

It broke my heart.

I hoped.

I held onto every conversation we'd shared.

I tried convincing myself to move on.

But every road somehow led back to you.

Even if this wasn't going to become the love story I dreamed of, I wanted you to know one thing...

I'd still be here.

Choosing You

Then something changed.

The uncertainty slowly became hope.

Hope became trust.

Trust became love.

I realised I didn't want anyone else.

Every heartbeat brought me back to you.

Every dream ended with you.

Every future I imagined had you standing beside me.

You weren't just someone I loved.

You had become home.

Completely, Hopelessly In Love

Somewhere along the way...

I stopped falling.

Because I'd already landed.

You became my peace.

My safe place.

My biggest smile.

My calm.

My passion.

My best friend.

The woman who made me believe love could still exist exactly the way I'd always dreamed it could.

You became the first thought in my mornings and the last thought before I fell asleep.

You became my favourite person.

My Promise

Love isn't only about the easy days.

It's about choosing each other when life hurts.

When grief arrives.

When fear takes over.

When one of us is struggling.

That's who I want to be.

I won't try to fix you.

I'll simply love you.

I'll hold your hand through the storms.

I'll celebrate your victories.

I'll sit beside you in silence when words aren't enough.

I'll protect your heart as carefully as you've trusted me with it.

Today

Today, when I look back at every song on this playlist, I don't just hear music.

I hear the story of a man whose heart slowly found its way home.

Every song reminds me of another step that led me to you.

If I had to live this story all over again...

I'd still choose you.

Every single time.

Because somewhere between the first smile, the first coffee, the first late-night conversation, and every song in between...

You became the greatest love I've ever known.

And if my heart could speak without music...

It would simply say

I love you, Journee. Yesterday. Today. Tomorrow. Always.

Part One — The First Spark

Smile Got Me Wild

Meeting you felt like a switch had been flipped inside me.

Before you, life was moving the way it always had—same days, same routines, same noise. But after that first meeting, everything felt louder somehow. Brighter. Like the world had suddenly turned the volume up.

That's what Smile Got Me Wild became for me.

I'd play it over and over, and every single time it came on, it was you in my head.

Your smile.

Your energy.

The way you made something in me wake up.

You had this way of getting under my skin so quickly, and I don't think you even knew it. I'd catch myself grinning like an idiot after our conversations, replaying little things you said, the sound of your laugh, the way you carried yourself.

You drove me wild.

And not just in the obvious way.

Yes, I wanted to pull you aside and kiss you. I wanted to feel what it would be like to be close to you, to close the space between us and stop pretending I wasn't thinking about it.

But underneath all that desire was something deeper.

Something quieter.

Because even then, before we had become anything, I could already feel the shape of what us might look like.

And that scared me.

Not because it felt wrong.

But because it felt possible.

So I told myself to slow down.

Play it cool.

Get to know you.

Because something in me knew that if this became real, it wouldn't be temporary.

And for the first time in a long time…

I wanted something real.

Sometimes it’s in the long talks until sunrise.

Sometimes it’s in the quiet way someone stays.

And you stayed.

Through the uncertainty.

Through the heavy days.

Through the healing.

Through the moments where love could have been easier to run from.

But we didn’t run.

We held on.

And because of that, what we built became something stronger than I ever imagined.

If I had to live this whole story again—

every song,

every feeling,

every ache,

every wait,

every lesson—

I would still choose you.

Every single time.

Because somewhere between the first smile, the first spark, the first time you made my heart race, and every song that followed…

you became the greatest love I’ve ever known.

And now, after all of it, after every chapter and every note…

there’s only one truth left.

If my heart could speak without music,

if it could strip away every lyric and every melody and leave only what’s real…

it would still say the same thing.

I love you, Journee.

I loved you in the beginning before I understood it.

I love you now with everything I am.

And I’ll love you in all the tomorrows still waiting for us.

Yesterday.

Today.

Tomorrow.

Always.
